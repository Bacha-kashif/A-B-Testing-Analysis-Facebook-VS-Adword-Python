## Project Overview
This project analyzes the performance of two digital advertising platforms: **Facebook Ads and Google AdWords**. The objective is to determine which platform performs better in terms of **clicks, conversions, and cost-effectiveness**.

Using Python and data analysis techniques, the **project performs exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, statistical analysis, and regression modeling** to evaluate advertising performance over time.

The analysis helps marketing teams make **data-driven decisions** about where to allocate advertising budgets for maximum return on investment (ROI).


## Business Problem

Marketing agencies often run campaigns on multiple advertising platforms. However, determining **which platform produces better results** can be difficult without proper data analysis.

The key business challenge is:
* Identify which advertising platform **delivers higher conversions**
* Understand the **distribution and frequency of conversions**
* Evaluate the **effectiveness of each campaign**
* Optimize **marketing budget allocation**

By solving this problem, companies can invest more in the **most profitable advertising channel**.

## 6. Research Questions
The project investigates several important marketing questions:

**1.** Which advertising platform generates **more clicks?**

**2.** Which platform produces **higher conversions?**

**3.** How are conversions **distributed across days?**

**4.** How frequently do **high-conversion days** occur?

**5.** Is there a **statistically significant difference** between Facebook and AdWords campaign performance?

**6.** Can advertising performance be **modeled using regression analysis**?


## Key Findings
### 1. Facebook Ads Generate More Conversions

The analysis shows that **Facebook Ads consistently produce higher conversions compared to AdWords**.

### 2. Distribution of Clicks and Conversions

The histogram analysis indicates:
* Both platforms have **fairly balanced distributions**
* No strong skewness or extreme outliers
* Most observations fall within the **mid-range conversion levels**

### 3. High Conversion Days

Conversion category analysis shows:
* Facebook has **more days with high conversion counts**
* AdWords has more **low to moderate conversion days**

### 4. Conversion Frequency

When categorizing conversions:
* Facebook frequently appears in **higher conversion categories**
* AdWords often appears in **lower conversion categories**

### 5. Campaign Performance Pattern

The visualization results suggest:
* Facebook campaigns maintain **more consistent high performance**
* AdWords campaigns show less **frequent peaks in conversions**


## Final Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are made:

### 1. Allocate More Budget to Facebook Ads

Since Facebook shows higher conversions, marketers should consider **increasing the advertising budget for Facebook campaigns**.

### 2. Optimize AdWords Campaign Strategy

AdWords campaigns may require:
* Improved **keyword targeting**
* Better **ad copy optimization**
* More **refined audience targeting**

### 3. Use Data-Driven Marketing Decisions**

Marketing teams should continuously analyze campaign data to ensure that resources are allocated to **high-performing channels**.

### 4. Implement Predictive Modeling**

Future campaigns can benefit from **machine learning models** to predict conversions and optimize marketing strategies.
